Meet My Family!

In my immediate family, I have a mom, a dad, an older sister, and a younger sister. My dad's parents also live in Highland Park, while my mom's parents live in New York. Read the information below to get to know my family!

This is my Mom, Gail. She was born in Johannesburg, South Africa. She and her family moved to the United States in 1977. My mom went to Tufts University to obtain her undergraduate degree and then for medical school. She has been a Radiologist for the past 20+ years. She is the best!

This is my Dad, Joel. He was born in Highland Park, Illinois. He went to University of Illinois-Urbana Chaimpaign and obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Psychology. After college, he became a Trader and worked in Chicago. Now, he is a mortgage broker. My Dad is such a funny and loving person.

This is my older sister, Sarah. She is only 18 months older than me, so we are very close. She is currently a senior at Washington University in St. Louis, studying political science. Sarah is such a passionate and kind person, and she loves to sing and perform.

This is my younger sister, Julia. She is a freshman here at the University of Michigan! It has been so fun having her here. We go to dinner every week to catch each other up on the past few days and study together a lot. Julia lives in South Quad and is loving UMich!

This is Granny Kay, my Mom's Mom. She is from South Africa and now lives int the suburbs of New York. She absolutely loves to play bridge and to drink tea. Although she is currently retired, she used to be an Anethesiologist.

This is Poppy, my Mom's Dad. He is also from South Africa, and has been a Urologist for over 50 years! He is finally retiring this upcoming year, but loves his work so much! I really admire him. My grandpa loves to do yoga and to play card games.

This is Grammie, my Dad's Mom. She is from Chicago and used to be a therapist. She is the best baker ever! Grammie makes the most delicious challah, brownies, mandel bread, and cake. It's always fun visiting her.

This is Boopa, my Dad's Dad. He is from Chicago and used to be an engineer. He is the sweetest, most generous person and spoils me and my cousins rotten! He loves to swim and to take us to dinner.
